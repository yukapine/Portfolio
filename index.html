About Me

Hello! My name is Jackson Pine and I am a Junior Computer Science Major at Boston University. My work includes software development, graphic design, illustration, and 3D modelling/sculpting. In my free time I work on creating interactive art pieces that allow for novel forms of engagement. I try to approach all my projects with a sense of exploration.

While my main goal is to work in software engineering, my appreciation for the physical world is just as strong as my attachment to digital creation. I find most of my inspiration in product, fashion, architectural, and furniture design, and I like to incorporate aspects of each into my work. If you're looking for an extra dimension of creativity and engagement in your projects, I am currently looking for work.
