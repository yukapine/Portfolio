About Me

Hello! My name is Jackson Pine and I am a Junior Computer Science Major at Boston University. My work includes illustration, graphic design, 3D modelling/sculpting, and software development. In my free time I work on creating interactive art pieces that allow for novel forms of engagement. I try to approach all my projects with a senses of exploration.

While my main goal is to work in software design, my appreciation for the physical world is just as strong as my attachment to digital creation. I find inspiration in product, fashion, architectural, and furniture design, and I like to incorporate aspects of each into my work. If you're looking for an extra dimension of creativity and engagement in your projects, I am currently looking for work.
