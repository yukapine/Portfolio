About Me

As a multidisciplinary creative professional, I blend the roles of artist, graphic designer, 3D modeller, and software developer. My passion lies in creating interactive art pieces that challenge conventional boundaries and explore the unusual, always seeking to push the envelope of digital expression.

My creative vision extends beyond the digital realm into the physical world, encompassing a deep appreciation for architecture, product design, and interior spaces. I find inspiration in the intersection of furniture design and fashion, where form meets function in unexpected ways. This diverse range of interests fuels my approach to creating unique, immersive experiences that bridge multiple creative disciplines.
